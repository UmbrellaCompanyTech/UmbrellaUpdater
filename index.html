Firmware Upload (read from USB device)

How to use

1. Unplug the power cable.
2. Connect the pedal to your PC using a USB-C cable.
3. While holding down both footswitch, power on the pedal.
4. Click the "Connect" button.
5. Select "FS in DFU mode" from the list.
6. Click the "Select Firmware" button and choose the firmware.
7. Click the "Upload" button.
8. Wait until “Done” is displayed.
9. The pedal will reboot automatically and then your pedal is now updated!

1. ペダルの電源を切る
2. USB CケーブルでPCと接続する
3. 両方のペダルのフットスイッチを押しながら、電源を入れる
4. 「Connect」ボタンを押す
5. 「DFU in FS Mode」を選択し接続する
6. 「Select Firmware」ボタンを押し、ファームウェアを選択する
7. 「Upload」ボタンを押し、ファームウェアをアップロードする
8. 「Done」と表示されるまで待つ
9. ペダルが自動的に再起動したら完了!

Prerequisites

In order to run this demo, you will need:

  • An up-to-date version of Chrome, at least version 61 or newer

  • A USB DFU class device

    For a WebUSB friendly USB DFU bootloader that can be flashed onto inexpensive STM32F103 boards, check out the dapboot project.

    Some recent STM32 parts have on-chip USB DfuSe bootloaders that are supported by this site.
